Machine Learning Engineer with 3+ years of experience specializing in computer vision. I lead cross-functional AI projects and optimize models for edge devices, with research interests in self-supervised vision and vision-language models.

Currently, I am working as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Dcube, Islamabad, where I develop and deploy end-to-end systems for real-time detection, recognition, and NLP-powered enterprise solutions. Previously, I was a Research Intern at the Computer Vision and Mixed Reality Lab, RheinMain University of Applied Sciences (Germany), working on transformer-based super-resolution for remote sensing imagery, and a Research Assistant at the TUKL NUST R&D Lab, focusing on generative models for signature verification.

I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Pakistan, where my thesis focused on Offline Handwritten Signature Generation Using Generative Models (Supervisor: Dr. Faisal Shafait). I earned my B.S. in Software Engineering (Gold Medalist) from BUITEMS, Pakistan.
