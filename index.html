Computer vision researcher working on deep learning for remote sensing, with a focus on trustworthy super-resolution of satellite imagery. My research interests span remote sensing, trustworthy remote sensing super-resolution, and computer vision, with a growing interest in reliable and uncertainty-aware geospatial AI.

Currently, I am a Research Assistant at the TUKL NUST R&D Lab, SEECS, NUST, working on deep learning for remote sensing super-resolution and computer vision, while also serving as a Machine Learning Research Engineer at Dcube, Islamabad. Previously, I was a Research Intern at the Computer Vision and Mixed Reality Lab, RheinMain University of Applied Sciences (Germany), working on transformer-based super-resolution for remote sensing imagery.

I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from the National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST), Pakistan, where my thesis focused on Offline Handwritten Signature Generation Using Generative Models (Supervisor: Dr. Faisal Shafait). I earned my B.S. in Software Engineering (Gold Medalist) from BUITEMS, Pakistan.

I am applying for PhD positions starting Fall 2027.
